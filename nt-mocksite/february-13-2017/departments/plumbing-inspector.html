Licenses - All plumbing contractors working in the city must be licensed.

Applications for licenses are available on this website as well as in our office. Fee for Master Plumbers are $125 and Journeyman is $75 all licenses are good for 1 year renewable ever December.

In addition to liability insurance, you must have NYS Worker's Compensation and NTS Disability or a NYS Disability Waiver.

Plumbing Exams - are given twice a year on the first Monday in November and the first Monday in April. Applications are available in our office or on this website. Deadline for exam applications is August 31st for the November exam and January 31st for the April exam. Exam correction is done by the Plumbing Board on the first Monday in December and May. Applicants will be notified within 7 - 10 business days. If you wish to discuss your results you can call our office and make an appointment to meet with the Plumbing Board at their next meeting.

Exam Fees - November Exams, Masters $100 and Journetman $50. April Exam, Master $250 and Journeyman $100.
